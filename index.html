How to Play

Objective: Take control of the board by eliminating all opponents' orbs.

  • Rule 1: Played on a customizable board (default 9x6).
  • Rule 2: Critical mass = adjacent cells (4 for middle, 3 for edges, 2 for corners).
  • Rule 3: Place an orb in an empty cell or one containing your color.
  • Rule 4: Reaching critical mass causes an explosion, distributing orbs to neighbors.
  • Rule 5: Explosions capture opponent orbs, converting them to your color!
  • Rule 6: If you lose all your orbs, you're out. The last player standing wins.

Advanced Winning Strategies

Want to dominate your friends? Keep these tips in mind:

  • Control the Corners: Corners only require a mass of 2 to explode. Securing them early gives you a cheap, highly defensive staging ground.
  • The Edge Advantage: Edges (mass 3) are your highways. Build along the edges to surround opponents trapped in the middle (mass 4).
  • The Domino Effect: Don't just build randomly. Stack your orbs so that one explosion triggers another adjacent critical cell, sweeping the board in a single turn.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How do you win the Chain Reaction game?

The sole objective is to eliminate all opponents from the board. You win by capturing your opponents' orbs through explosive chain reactions until you are the last player with orbs remaining on the grid.

What is 'critical mass' in Chain Reaction?

Critical mass refers to the maximum number of orbs a cell can hold before it destabilizes and explodes. Corners have a critical mass of 2, edges have 3, and all inner cells have a critical mass of 4. When a cell reaches critical mass, it bursts, sending its orbs to adjacent cells.

Can I play Chain Reaction on my mobile phone?

Yes! This game is fully optimized for touch screens and mobile browsers. Just share the link with your friends, and you can pass the phone around to play.
